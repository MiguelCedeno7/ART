Evens Saint Clair

Born in Haiti, raised in Chicago, and later relocating to Miami, Florida, Evens Saint Clair is an artist, designer, and visionary who seamlessly blends art and luxury fashion.

His unique creations—ranging from Art Bags to paintings and fine jewelry—have captivated a select group of collectors and high-net-worth individuals worldwide. Unaffiliated with any traditional artistic movement.

Evens Saint Clair is a true outsider artist and his work is deeply personal, inspired by his life experiences, extensive travels, and meticulous craftsmanship.
